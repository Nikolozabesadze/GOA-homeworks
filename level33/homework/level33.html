About Cakes

Cakes are sweet baked desserts, usually made with flour, sugar, and some type of fat. They can be flavored with various ingredients and come in a multitude of styles.

Simple Cake Recipe

Vanilla Cake

Ingredients: 2 cups flour, 1 ½ cups sugar, ½ cup butter, 1 cup milk, 3 eggs, 2 tsp baking powder, vanilla extract.

Instructions: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Pour into a greased cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
